# Search Criteria

Plain-English rules for what to surface in the weekly digest. When in doubt, lean toward including borderline matches. Over-filtering loses signal; a slightly long digest is easy to scan.

## Roles to Include

### Technical Art

Any senior level: Senior, Staff, Principal, Lead, Manager, Director. Title varies a lot across studios and tech companies. Include if the work involves bridging art and engineering, tools development, pipeline work, shader work, real-time graphics, or character / asset systems.

### Engineering Management

EM, Senior EM, or Director-level roles managing teams in:

- Graphics, rendering, or 3D systems
- Tools, pipeline, or infrastructure for content creation
- ML / AI research engineering
- Robotics, simulation, or synthetic data
- Research engineering broadly

### Senior IC Software Engineering

Staff, Principal, or Senior Staff roles in (highest-relevance domains first):

- **3D computer graphics, animation systems, and character systems** — the strongest fit. Examples: rigging, deformation, cloth/hair/fur, animation tooling, character pipelines, real-time character rendering.
- Graphics tools and pipelines (working *with* graphics APIs and engines, not implementing them at the kernel level)
- 3D content production tools and asset pipelines
- Real-time perception, simulation, or synthetic data generation
- ML / AI tooling and pipelines (application layer, not low-level training infrastructure)
- Research engineering supporting graphics or ML work

### Research Engineer

When the work intersects with 3D, graphics, animation, characters, robotics, simulation, or synthetic data. Pure NLP / LLM research is not a fit unless it has a multimodal, vision, or 3D angle.

**"Research Scientist" / "Applied Scientist" titles are usually too stretchy** — these are typically PhD-level pure-research roles, far from Dave's tools/pipeline/engineering background. Surface only if the title or description explicitly anchors in 3D, graphics, animation, characters, robotics, or simulation. When in doubt, skip.

## Roles to Exclude

- Anything below Senior level: Junior, Associate, "I", "II", new-grad
- Internships
- Contract, contractor, or consultant roles
- Firmware, embedded systems, kernel, driver, or hardware-level roles
- Engine and renderer internals (writing the rendering engine at a low level, not using one)
- Pure compute infrastructure or data platform roles
- Recruiter, sourcer, or talent acquisition roles
- Roles requiring relocation outside the location criteria below

## Location

Acceptable in this order of preference:

1. Fully remote, US-based ("remote, anywhere" or "remote, US")
2. Hybrid in the Seattle metro (Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland)
3. Onsite in the Seattle metro

Decline: onsite-only roles requiring relocation to Bay Area, LA, NYC, or anywhere else. A role listing multiple offices that include a Seattle option is fine. A role listing "remote, must reside in California" or similar geographic restriction outside WA is not.

## Compensation Floor

$100k base salary minimum. This is a low placeholder mostly to catch obvious junior roles that slipped past other filters. Most senior roles will not list comp publicly. Do not exclude based on missing comp data, only on explicitly low listed comp.

This floor will rise over time. For now leave it where it is so the digest captures the full shape of the market.

## Calibration Notes

- Currently 54, M2-equivalent Manager at a large tech research org. Comfortable seeing peer-level and one-step-up roles.
- Open to both IC return (Staff or Principal) and continued management. Surface both equally; do not optimize for one.
- Background is technical art and tools / pipeline engineering with strong Python and graphics fundamentals. ML and AI are an area of expansion, not core experience yet, so ML roles should lean toward "ML applied to my domains" rather than "ML researcher from scratch."
- **Highest-affinity domains are 3D computer graphics, animation systems, and character / rigging systems.** Lean in on these — surface borderline matches in these domains over borderline matches in adjacent areas.
- Avoiding being ring-fenced is a real preference. Roles with broader scope (cross-team, cross-discipline, ambiguous) are usually a better fit than narrow-spec roles. Note this in the relevance blurb when it applies.
- Earliest realistic move date is Q1 2027. This search is passive-but-serious: surfacing the shape of the market matters more than urgency on any one role.

## Output Per Posting

For each posting that passes, include in the digest:

- Tier (T1, T2, T3, or T4)
- Company
- Title
- Location (remote / hybrid / onsite, plus city if relevant)
- Direct apply link
- One sentence on why it is relevant (what about this posting matched the criteria above)
