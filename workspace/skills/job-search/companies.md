# Target Companies

Curated list for the weekly job search, organized by tier so the digest can be scanned by relevance. Add or prune as priorities shift. One line per company.

URLs are starting points. If a company has migrated their ATS or moved their careers page, follow the canonical link from their root domain.

Meta is intentionally excluded (current employer; internal moves are a separate process).

Pure VFX / animation studios (Pixar, WDAS, DreamWorks, Netflix Animation, Sony Pictures Imageworks, Weta FX, DNEG, Framestore, MPC) are intentionally excluded — they are predominantly on-site Emeryville / LA / Vancouver / London / NZ, and Dave is not relocating for production work. Re-add specific R&D groups (e.g. ILM StageCraft) if they appear independently.
